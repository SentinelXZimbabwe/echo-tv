How to Create a PayNow Account

  1. Visit paynow.co.zw
  2. Click Sign Up
  3. Choose Individual or Business
  4. Complete registration and verify your details
  5. Link EcoCash, bank account, or card

Once payment is completed, your Token ID will be activated automatically and linked to your EchoTV account, granting full access to the platform.
